# Brand Guidelines

## Visual Identity

Our visual language reflects our "Not a tour. A vibe." philosophy. It is modern, vibrant, and authentic, utilizing a **Glassmorphism** aesthetic to convey transparency and depth.

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Tirak Pink** (`#FF2E63`): Used for primary actions, highlights, and energy. Represents passion and fun.
- **Tirak Purple** (`#6C63FF`): Used for gradients, backgrounds, and depth. Represents magic and connection.

#### Backgrounds
- **Glass**: Semi-transparent white/gray with blur effects (`backdrop-blur-xl`).
- **Gradient**: Soft gradients blending pink and purple for emotional resonance.

### Typography

- **Headings**: Clean, bold sans-serif (Inter/SF Pro) for readability and modernity.
- **Body**: Highly legible sans-serif with comfortable line height.

### Iconography

We use **Lucide React** icons for a consistent, clean, and stroke-based look.

## Voice & Tone

See full [Voice & Tone Documentation](/docs/03-audience/voice-and-tone) for detailed examples.

### Brand Personality
**If Tirak were a person:**
- **Age:** Late 20s.
- **Personality:** Confident, warm, slightly irreverent friend who knows all the best spots.
- **Style:** Casual but not sloppy. Authentic but not chaotic.
- **Voice:** "Look, I get that platforms need to make money. But 30% commission? That's not a partnership, that's a tax."

### Core Values
1. **Fairness**: 15-20% commission (vs 30-40%).
2. **Authenticity**: Real people, real connections.
3. **Safety**: Verification and support.
4. **Autonomy**: Companions control their business.
5. **Community**: We support each other.

### Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| **Be Real**: Use natural language. | **Be Corporate**: Use jargon like "synergy". |
| **Be Empowering**: Focus on companion earnings. | **Be Paternalistic**: Act like we're saving them. |
| **Be Fun**: Use emojis and casual phrasing. | **Be Sloppy**: Ignore grammar or clarity. |
| **Be Direct**: Explain fees clearly. | **Be Evasive**: Hide costs in fine print. |
