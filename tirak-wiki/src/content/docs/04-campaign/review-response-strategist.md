## Review Response Strategy

### Five Star Strategy

#### Principles

- Acknowledge and thank the reviewer enthusiastically, using a conversion-focused tone.
- Echo specific positive points mentioned in the review, reinforcing key product benefits and value proposition.
- Subtly encourage future engagement or repeat experiences, turning satisfaction into loyalty.
- Tailor language to resonate with both Companion and Traveler personas, addressing their unique motivations.
- Highlight aspects relevant to ALL 9 TIRAK categories (Culture, Adventure, Wellness, Nightlife, Lifestyle, Private, Cinema, Events, Food & Drink) where applicable, showcasing the breadth of offerings.

### One Star Strategy

#### Principles

- Acknowledge the negative experience and express sincere regret and apology for not meeting expectations.
- Empathize with their specific complaint, demonstrating understanding.
- Offer a clear, private path to resolution (e.g., direct contact via email/phone) to address concerns offline.
- Reiterate commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, reassuring future customers.
- Avoid defensiveness or making excuses, focusing on problem-solving.
- Tailor language to address Companion and Traveler concerns, recognizing their distinct impacts.
- Address specific TIRAK category issues where relevant, showing attention to detail.

### Troll Strategy

#### Identification Criteria

- Lack of specific details or vague, unsubstantiated complaints.
- Use of offensive, abusive, discriminatory, or hateful language.
- Reviews that are clearly off-topic, irrelevant to the product/service, or about a different business.
- Repetitive posting of the same review across multiple platforms or by multiple accounts.
- Reviews from anonymous or suspicious profiles with no prior activity or a history of similar negative reviews for other businesses.
- Content that appears to be fabricated, based on misinformation, or directly contradicts verifiable facts.
- Direct personal attacks against staff or the brand without substantive operational feedback.

#### Approach

**Report If Applicable**: If the review clearly violates platform terms of service (e.g., hate speech, spam, harassment, privacy violations), the primary action is to report it immediately to the platform administrator for removal. Do not respond publicly if reporting is the primary and most effective action.

**Respond If Necessary**: If reporting is not viable (e.g., the review is vague but not malicious enough for removal) or if a public response is deemed strategically necessary to show responsiveness, respond neutrally and professionally. Avoid engaging in arguments or validating baseless claims.

**Neutral Response Template**: We're sorry to hear your experience wasn't satisfactory. We strive to provide excellent service and value all feedback. If you'd like to share more details about your visit, please contact us directly at [Customer Service Email/Phone] so we can understand and address any legitimate concerns.

##### Key Principles

- Do not personalize the response or get defensive; maintain a professional and calm tone.
- Keep the response brief, factual, and generic if specific details are absent.
- Shift the conversation offline to a private channel if possible, preventing public escalation.
- Maintain brand integrity and professionalism, demonstrating a commitment to genuine customer service to other potential customers.
