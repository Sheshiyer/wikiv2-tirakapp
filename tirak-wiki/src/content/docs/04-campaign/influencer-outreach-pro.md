## Influencer Outreach Strategy

### Ideal Influencer Profile

**Micro Vs Macro**: Micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) are prioritized due to lean budget, higher engagement, and niche audience alignment. Macro-influencers considered for broader awareness if specific budget allows.

#### Specific Niches

- Travel (destination guides, unique local experiences, solo/couples travel)
- Lifestyle (daily routines, hobbies, luxury, digital nomad, personal growth)
- Food & Drink (restaurant/bar reviews, culinary experiences, cooking classes)
- Wellness (yoga, meditation, spa, retreats, healthy living)
- Adventure/Outdoor (hiking, extreme sports, unique outdoor activities)
- Culture/Arts (museums, historical tours, local traditions, festivals, galleries)
- Nightlife/Events (concerts, club reviews, festival guides, unique evening experiences)
- Cinema/Entertainment (film reviews, movie-themed experiences, local cinema events)
- Private/Luxury Experiences (exclusive tours, bespoke services, high-end travel)

**Audience Overlap**: Influencers whose audience aligns with both 'Travelers' (seeking experiences) and 'Companions' (offering/sharing experiences) or who can speak to both sides of the platform.

### Value Hooks

- Monetary Compensation: Competitive rates, flat fees, or commission-based structures.
- Exclusive Access: Early access to premium Tirak features, unique experiences, or new destinations.
- Audience Relevance: High-quality, engaging content opportunities directly relevant to their audience's interests in travel and unique experiences.
- Content Creation Opportunities: Tirak provides a rich source of authentic, local, and diverse experiences for compelling content.
- Brand Enhancement: Position as a curator of unique experiences and a leader in authentic travel/local engagement.
- Partnership Potential: Opportunity for long-term collaboration and ambassadorship with a growing platform.

### Channel Adaptation

#### Dm Strategy

**Description**: Concise, personalized, and direct. Start with specific content praise, introduce Tirak briefly, state the value, and drive to email for details. Use emojis appropriately.

**Length Guideline**: 2-4 sentences.

#### Email Strategy

**Description**: Professional, detailed, and structured. Clear subject line, comprehensive introduction to Tirak, specific partnership details, compensation, and clear CTA for a call or dedicated link.

**Length Guideline**: 150-250 words.

### Follow Up Sequence

**Step**: Initial Outreach

**Type**: DM/Email

**Description**: First contact, introduces Tirak, outlines the opportunity, and highlights the value hook. Personalized and direct.

---

**Step**: Bump/Reminder

**Type**: DM/Email

**Delay**: 3-5 business days

**Description**: Gentle follow-up. Reiterate value, perhaps add a new angle or urgency, or simply ask if they received the initial message.

---

**Step**: Final Attempt

**Type**: DM/Email

**Delay**: 5-7 business days after bump

**Description**: Last push. Polite, acknowledges busy schedule, offers a clear 'no pressure' out, but leaves the door open for future collaboration.
