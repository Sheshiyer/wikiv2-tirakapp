**The problem:**

**The fix:**

### 3. They Used Keywords Travelers Search For

**The problem:**  
Your experience titles are vague ("Fun Bangkok Tour"). Travelers search specific keywords ("street food," "bar crawl," "temple walk"). You don't match. You don't show up.

**The fix:**  
👉 **Use exact keywords in experience titles:**
- ❌ "Fun Bangkok Tour"

- ❌ "Night Out Experience"
- ✅ "Khao San Bar Crawl: Rooftop Bars + Live Music (5 Hours)"

**Pro tip from Som (6 bookings/month):**

👉 **[Edit Your Experience Titles]**

### 4. They Priced Competitively (You Might Be Too High)

**The problem:**  
New companions with 0 reviews pricing at ฿2,500 (same as 5-star companions with 30 reviews). Travelers skip you. They book someone with proof of quality.

**The fix:**  
👉 **Start competitive, raise rates later:**
- **0-5 reviews:** ฿1,200-1,500 (build trust fast)
- **6-15 reviews:** ฿1,500-2,000 (you're proven)
- **16+ reviews:** ฿2,000-2,500+ (you're premium)

**Pro tip from Kai (15 bookings/month):**
> "I started at ฿1,200 for street food tours. Got 5 bookings in 2 weeks, all 5-star reviews. Then raised to ฿1,800. Now I charge ฿2,200 and I'm booked every weekend. Start low, build reputation, raise rates."

👉 **[Adjust Your Pricing]**  
(Link: https://tirak.com/dashboard/pricing)

### 5. They Responded to Inquiries Within 1 Hour

**The problem:**  
Traveler messages you at 2pm. You reply at 10pm. They've already booked someone else. You lost ฿1,500.

**The fix:**  
👉 **Turn on mobile notifications** (get instant alerts when travelers message you)  
👉 **Reply within 1 hour** (faster response = higher booking rate)  
👉 **Use templates** (save pre-written responses for common questions)

**Pro tip from Mint (10 bookings/month):**
