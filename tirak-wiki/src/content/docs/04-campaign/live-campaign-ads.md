## Live Campaign Ads

**Motif**: Discount Offer

**Persona Focus**: Both Companion & Traveler

**Category Focus**: Across all 9 categories (Culture, Adventure, Wellness, Nightlife, Lifestyle, Private, Cinema, Events, Food & Drink)

**Narrative Summary**: The 'Discount Offer' motif is strategically designed to drive immediate conversions by appealing to the inherent human desire for value and savings. For the **Traveler** persona, these ads highlight the opportunity to explore more with less, perhaps securing a longer adventure trip, a premium wellness package, or access to multiple cultural events within budget. The dynamic `{{funding_percentage}}` token directly quantifies the financial benefit, making the offer tangible. For the **Companion** persona, the discount translates into shared experiences becoming more accessible or enabling a more luxurious shared moment, such as a discounted private dining experience or a special cinema night for two. The ad copy explicitly weaves in references to **all 9 categories**: from 'romantic private cinema night' and 'exhilarating adventure' to 'serene wellness retreat' and 'gourmet food & drink events.' This ensures broad appeal regardless of the user's primary interest. The conversion-focused tone is achieved through phrases like 'Don't miss out!', 'Book now,' and 'Claim your discount,' creating a sense of urgency tied to the `{{deadline_date}}` token. The `{{perks_description}}` token further enhances perceived value, offering an additional incentive beyond the discount itself. This lean budget approach maximizes impact by focusing on a clear, compelling financial incentive across diverse interests and user needs.
