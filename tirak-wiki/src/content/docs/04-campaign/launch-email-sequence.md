## Dynamic Fields Guide

**First Name**: Recipient's first name, for personalization.

**Launch Offer Details**: Specific details about the launch offer (e.g., '20% off all first bookings').

**Cta Link**: The URL for the primary Call to Action.

**Extension Duration**: The length of the extension for the launch offers (e.g., 'another 48 hours').

**Press Quote 1**: A compelling quote from a media outlet about TIRAK's offerings.

**Press Quote 2**: A second compelling quote from a media outlet about TIRAK's offerings.

**Current Bookings Number**: An impressive number reflecting successful bookings during the launch week (e.g., '1,500+').

**Launch Period End Date**: The specific date when the launch offers will officially conclude.
