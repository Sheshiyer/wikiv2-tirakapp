1. Vendor Database (Master)

~/tirak_thailand_vendors.json

608 verified vendors across 10 categories. Each entry: name, category, URL, location, email, phone, source. All URLs verified live.

+ 10 per-category CSV exports

2. Contact Enrichment Scraper

~/enrich_vendor_contacts.py

Automated scraper that visits vendor websites, extracts emails + phone numbers, filters junk, and updates the master JSON. Re-runnable.

Result: +127 emails, +233 phones

3. Outreach Emails (608 files)

~/tirak_outreach_emails/*.txt

Category-specific personalized email for every vendor. 10 unique templates with tailored hooks, value props, and social proof per category.

249 ready to send today

4. Email Templates & Summary

~/tirak_email_templates.json
~/tirak_outreach_summary.csv

Raw templates for bulk mail tools + CSV with vendor name, email, category, status for CRM import.

5. "Why Tirak" One-Pager

~/tirak_why_tirak_onepager.html

Professional HTML pitch document with commission comparison table, benefit cards, 10 category tags, and CTA. Print-ready.

Brand colors: gold #e8a838 / navy #1a1a2e

6. WhatsApp/LINE Scripts

~/tirak_whatsapp_scripts.md

3-touch messaging sequence (intro, value follow-up, social proof nudge) with category-specific variations for all 10 verticals.

454 vendors reachable by phone

7. Objection Handling Cheat Sheet

~/tirak_objection_handling.md

15 common vendor objections with scripted rebuttals. Covers commission, trust, effort, logistics, and category-specific concerns. Plus closing phrases.

8. Outreach Email Generator

~/tirak_outreach_emails.py

Re-runnable Python script that reads vendor JSON and generates personalized emails. Update templates and re-run anytime vendor data changes.
