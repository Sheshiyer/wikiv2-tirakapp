# Tirak Vendor Objection Handling — Quick Reference

Use these scripted rebuttals during phone/WhatsApp outreach. Each objection comes from the Tirak brand wiki research on real vendor pain points.

---

## Commission & Pricing

### "15-20% commission seems too good to be true."
> **Rebuttal:** "No catch. We're bootstrapped and lean — no fancy offices in Amsterdam like Withlocals. We prioritize your earnings over platform profits. 15-20% covers payment processing, insurance, and platform maintenance. We publish the breakdown transparently."

### "I'm already on [Withlocals/Viator/Klook]. Why switch?"
> **Rebuttal:** "You don't have to switch — Tirak isn't exclusive. List on multiple platforms. But compare: Withlocals takes 30%, ToursByLocals takes 40%. On a ฿2,000 booking, you keep ฿1,700 with Tirak vs. ฿1,400 on Withlocals. That's ฿300 more per booking. Over 10 bookings a month, that's ฿3,000 extra in your pocket."

### "But bigger platforms have more travelers."
> **Rebuttal:** "Fair point. But we invest 80% of our marketing budget on bringing travelers to our vendors. We're Thailand-first — not a global platform where you compete with operators in 89 countries. And because our commission is lower, travelers pay less too — which means more bookings for you."

---

## Trust & Legitimacy

### "I've never heard of Tirak."
> **Rebuttal:** "We're new but growing fast — 600+ Thai vendors already across 10 categories. We're Thailand-focused, not trying to be everything to everyone. Think of us as the local platform that actually understands Thai tourism, not a European company expanding into Asia as an afterthought."

### "How do I know travelers are real / safe?"
> **Rebuttal:** "Every traveler is ID-verified before they can book. They have reviews from other vendors. You chat with them in-app before accepting — no personal phone number sharing. We have a report button, emergency contacts, and block list. Zero reported safety incidents so far."

### "What if Tirak shuts down?"
> **Rebuttal:** "We're not asking you to leave other platforms. List on Tirak AND keep your other listings. There's no exclusivity, no lock-in. If we disappear tomorrow, you've lost nothing. But if we grow — which is happening — you've gained a new channel of travelers."

## Effort & Setup

### "I don't have time for another platform."
> **Rebuttal:** "Setup takes 10 minutes. Literally. Upload a few photos (iPhone is fine), write a short description, set your rates, go live. No professional photoshoots, no video auditions, no reference letters. You can do it during a coffee break."

### "I don't have professional photos."
> **Rebuttal:** "Good — we actually prefer authentic photos. iPhone selfies at your restaurant, your spa, your tour bus? Perfect. Travelers want to see the real you, not a staged studio shoot. Our top-performing vendors use authentic photos."

### "What if I don't get any bookings?"
> **Rebuttal:** "80% of our marketing budget goes to finding travelers for you. New vendors get featured in our 'New on Tirak' section — you don't get buried behind established listings. Average new vendors see 2-3 bookings in their first 2 weeks."

---

## Logistics

### "How do payments work?"
> **Rebuttal:** "Travelers pay through the app. Your earnings hit your bank account within 24-48 hours. No cash handling, no chasing payments, no 'I'll Venmo you later' situations. Clean, secure, fast."

### "Can I control my schedule?"
> **Rebuttal:** "100%. Toggle availability on/off with one tap. Weekend-only? Fine. Random Tuesday off? Toggle off. Available tonight for last-minute bookings? Toggle on. You control everything."

### "Can I set my own prices?"
> **Rebuttal:** "Absolutely. Charge more on weekends, offer discounts for repeat customers, price premium for special experiences. Your rates, your rules. We never lock you into pricing tiers."

### "Do I need to respond to every inquiry?"
> **Rebuttal:** "No. You choose which bookings to accept. See the traveler's profile and reviews first, chat if you want, then decide. No obligation to accept everything."

## Category-Specific Objections

### Food operators: "Cookly already works for me."
> **Rebuttal:** "Keep Cookly! But Tirak's commission is 15-20% vs. Cookly's 25-30%. On a ฿2,000 class, that's ฿200-300 more per booking. Plus, Tirak travelers are looking for all food experiences — not just cooking classes. Street food tours, market visits, farm-to-table dinners."

### Hotels: "I'm already on Booking.com and Agoda."
> **Rebuttal:** "OTAs are great for rooms. But Tirak lets you list experiences — your rooftop bar event, your neighborhood food walk, your cultural stay package. Higher margin than room-only bookings, and you reach travelers who specifically want boutique over chain."

### Transport: "We have our own website and booking system."
> **Rebuttal:** "Keep using it! Tirak is an additional channel. We have travelers who are booking experiences AND need transport — airport transfers, ferry connections, day trips. They book everything in one place. Your service shows up at the exact moment they need it."

### MICE: "We get leads through our own sales team."
> **Rebuttal:** "Tirak supplements your existing sales — it doesn't replace it. We have corporate groups actively searching for MICE venues in Thailand. That's warm leads coming to you, not cold outreach. Zero risk to try."

## Closing Phrases

When you get a "maybe" or "let me think about it":

> "Totally understand. Here's what I'd suggest: take 10 minutes to set up a basic profile. If bookings come in, great — you've got a new revenue channel. If not, you've lost nothing. No commitment, no cost, no exclusivity."

> "I'll send you our one-pager so you can share it with your team. It has the commission comparison and all the details. Just reply 'interested' when you're ready and I'll send the setup link."

> "No pressure at all. The signup link is tirak.app/partners whenever you're ready. Takes 10 minutes. We'll be here."
