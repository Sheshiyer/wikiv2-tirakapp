## Key Rules for WhatsApp/LINE Outreach

1. **Never send more than 3 messages** — respect their time
2. **Space messages 3-4 days apart** — not spammy
3. **Keep each message under 200 words** — WhatsApp is for short messages
4. **Always include one clear CTA** — "reply interested" or "click this link"
5. **Use emojis sparingly** — 1-2 per message max, keeps it professional-casual
6. **Time messages for business hours** — 10am-5pm Thai time
7. **Personalize the vendor name** — never send a generic blast
8. **Use the category-specific hook** — shows you know their business
9. **If they respond "interested"** — send the one-pager PDF + signup link immediately
10. **If they say "not interested"** — thank them politely, remove from sequence
